Rating System

How are tournaments rated?

We take an simple average of all user submitted ratings to get a tournament's overall rating score.

The letter grades are an average of user submitted ratings too. When you submit a review, you can choose between a few options:
['Poor': 'F', 'Below Average': 'D', 'Average': 'C', 'Above Average': 'B', 'Excellent': 'A']
