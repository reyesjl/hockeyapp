Overview

Our platform facilitates user engagement through a comprehensive rating system encompassing over all ratings on a scale of 1.0 to 5.0 and quality ratings graded from A to F. Users can contribute to the community by submitting reviews for tournaments, restaurants, and entertainments. The rating calculations involve simple averages of user submissions.

Tournaments, restaurants, and entertainments undergo staff review processes before being publicly accessible, ensuring accuracy, completeness, and the prevention of duplicates. Users play a crucial role in shaping the platform's content, directly influencing the overall and quality ratings of the entities they review.
