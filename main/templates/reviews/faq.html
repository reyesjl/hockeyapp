Rating System

Q: How do ratings work here?

A: We have two kinds of ratings that empower users. This gives us accurate and detailed information to work with.

A: Our overall ratings are value between [1.0 - 5.0]

A: Our quality ratings are grade letters [A, B, C, D, F]

Q: How are tournaments rated?

A: We calculate a simple average from all user submitted overall ratings.

A: We also calculate a simple average for user submitted quality ratings.

A: Be aware that tournaments also have local restaurant/entertainment locations and reviews that are hockey-specific!

A: When you submit a review, you directly impact the tournament/restaurant/establishment and its viewers.

Tournament Reviews

Q: How do I add a new tournament review?
